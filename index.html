Spero Photography exists to spread the message of hope. Our focus is using architecture to tell these stories. Click below to see our latest project.

Long Journey Home

Long Journey Home 

Our latest project, Long Journey Home, sparked from a final project in my photography class. Choosing to shoot in the fine art category, I did not want to just take nice photos. I wanted the photos I took to tell a story and not just any story. I wanted the story to display our purpose here at Spero Photography, a story of hope. I hope these photos take you on the deep and meaningful story they are meant to tell. The journey may be rough, but home is in sight.
